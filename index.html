Stephanie Mecham

I am a dual-degree Master's candidate in the School of Information and School of Public Health at the University of Michigan, pursuing degrees in health informatics and global health epidemiology. My research interests are infectious disease transmission modeling, conducting data and statistical analysis on parasitic helminth diseases in low and middle income countries, and performing data analysis and visualization on health data. I currently am a Graduate Student Instructor in the Department of Biology at the University of Michigan, where I lead microbiology labs for Biology 207. My career interests are in data science and data analytics with specializations in healthcare and infectious disease.
